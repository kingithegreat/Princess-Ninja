## 2. Audience

The App is a stylized endless-runner intended for a **general audience
(13+)**. It is not directed to children and does not participate in Google
Play's Designed for Families program.

## 3. What information the App collects

**The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
information.** It runs fully on-device: it requests no internet access, uses
no cloud backend, includes no analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting
SDKs, and creates no accounts. It collects no names, email addresses, phone
numbers, location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data, and does
not profile or track users.

Game data — your earned currency, cosmetic unlocks (trail/cape recolors),
and run-modifier purchases (Second Wind, Head Start, Forgiving Combo, Steady
Pace, Gold Rush) — is stored **only on your device** via `localStorage` and
is removed when you uninstall the App. The Google Play Store separately
processes download and device information under Google's own privacy
policy.

## 4. No ads, no selling

The App shows no advertising of any kind and sells or shares no personal
information — there is none to sell or share. In-game currency is earned by
playing only; there are no real-money purchases in the current build.
